Aramm is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language political drama film directed by Gopi Nainar. The film features Nayanthara as a district collector, with Ramachandran Durairaj and Sunu Lakshmi in supporting roles 1. The film revolves around the story of a district collector named Madhivadhani who is struggling with her approval to a higher official and telling a bone-chilling story to him. The story begins with poor people fighting with the police for clean water, sanitation, and the verdict to arrest their cruel landlord. A woman named Sumathi and her 4-year-old daughter Dhanshika are walking together back home. Sumathi soon learns that Dhanshika went missing and enquires the villagers, who tell her that her daughter fell into a borewell.
